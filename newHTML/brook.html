Soul King Brook

Brook aka Dead Bones later Soul King is the musician of the Straw Hat Pirates and one of the main characters of One Piece. He is a skeleton that the Straw Hats found on board of a ghost ship after drifting into the Florian Triangle. He is a Devil Fruit user who ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi, and is undead, having been brought back into a pseudo-immortal state because of its powers. He is the ninth member of the Straw Hat crew, the eighth to join Luffy's crew and being the oldest member on board. He is also one of the two swordsmen onboard their current ship, with the other being Roronoa Zoro.
